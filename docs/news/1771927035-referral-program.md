Earn sats by sharing LNVPS! We've launched a referral program that pays you when someone orders a new VM using your referral link.

## How It Works

1. Head to **Account -> Referral Program** and enroll
2. Share your unique referral link with others
3. When someone visits your link and orders a new VM, you earn a payout

## Payout Options

Choose how you want to get paid:

- **Lightning Address** — Payouts sent directly to any Lightning Address
- **NWC Wallet** — Payouts sent via your configured Nostr Wallet Connect wallet

You can switch between payout methods at any time from the referral settings page.

## Track Your Earnings

Your referral dashboard shows everything at a glance: successful referrals, earnings broken down by currency, and a full payout history.

Get started today at **Account -> Referral Program** and start earning!
