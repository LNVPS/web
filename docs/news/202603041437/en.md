We have been shipping steadily since the referral program launched. Here is everything that has landed in the control panel since then.

## New Themes and Redesigned UI

The interface has been overhauled with two new themes: a **Neon** dark theme and a **Light** theme. You can toggle between themes at any time using the new theme switcher in the navigation bar. The VM list now displays your servers in a compact row layout that fits more information at a glance.

## Revolut Card Payments

In addition to Bitcoin Lightning and NWC, you can now pay using a **Revolut card** via the embedded Revolut checkout. The payment flow has been redesigned to show all available payment methods together, including processing fees so you know exactly what you will be charged before confirming.

## Flexible Renewal Intervals

When renewing or ordering a VM, you can now select from multiple billing intervals rather than being locked to a single period. Choose the interval that suits your needs and the price updates automatically.

## Contact Page

A new **Contact** page is available from the main navigation for general enquiries.

## IP Space Listing

Your allocated IP addresses and subnets are now visible in the account overview, making it easy to see all resources associated with your account in one place.

## CPU Information

VM cards and the resources page now display CPU manufacturer, architecture, and feature flags, giving you clearer visibility into the hardware your VMs are running on.

## Account Page Redesign

The account section has been reorganised with a **persistent sidebar** navigation. Each section (Settings, SSH Keys, Domains, Referral, Messages, Support) now has its own dedicated page with a clear label and active-state indicator.

## Nostr Domain Path Proxy

The Nostr domain configuration page now includes step-by-step instructions for setting up a **path-based proxy** (`/_nostr/...`) as an alternative to the direct subdomain configuration.

## Encrypted Direct Messages (NIP-17)

Support communicates via end-to-end encrypted direct messages using the NIP-17 standard. A dedicated **Messages** tab in the Account section lets you view and reply to messages from the support team. Messages are decrypted locally using your Nostr keys and cached in your browser for fast loading.

## Email Verification on Order

When placing a new VM order, an inline email verification step appears if your account email is not yet confirmed. This keeps the order flow in one place without redirecting you away.

## Reinstall on VM Detail Page

The reinstall action has been moved from the VM list to the individual **VM detail page**, alongside the other management actions.

## Internationalisation

The full control panel is now available in ten languages: Arabic, Chinese, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, and Turkish. Your browser's preferred language is detected automatically and you can switch at any time from the language selector in the navigation bar. Dates, numbers, and costs are formatted to match your selected locale, and Arabic is displayed with a right-to-left layout throughout.
